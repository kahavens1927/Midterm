Four generations of the Iaria Family have been serving antipasto, pizza, lasagna, & spaghetti on this corner for over 90 years.
Our pizza dough and sauce are still made from scratch. Our meatballs, Italian sausage, tiramisu, roquefort and all sauces are made in house.
Our staff is friendly and works hard to make our guests' experience both comfortable & enjoyable. Iaria's is retro to its core.
The exterior and interior still have original red & green neon and the walls are lined with celebrities, friends & family.
